PROJECTS (Selected)
--------------------

Project: Snowflake Data Platform Migration — Telio AS
Role: Technical Lead
Duration: March 2022 – November 2022
Team size: 4 engineers

Full description:
We had an Oracle-based DWH that was expensive, slow to query, and impossible
to scale elastically. I led the design and execution of a full migration to
Snowflake on AWS. The project involved:
- Business requirement gathering across 15 stakeholder groups
- Source system inventory (42 source systems identified)
- Schema mapping and semantic reconciliation
- dbt model build-out from scratch (no reuse of old stored procs)
- Historical data load (12 TB, approx. 7 years of history)
- Parallel running period (6 weeks) with automated reconciliation reports
- Cutover coordination and hypercare (4 weeks post-cutover)
Result: Query costs down 70%. Storage costs down 40%. BI refresh time
from 6h to 20min. Team NPS for data platform jumped from 22 to 67.
Technologies: Snowflake, dbt, Fivetran, Airflow, Python, Terraform, AWS
